Daily Expenses

Record any cash or non-cash expense incurred during your shift and submit it for manager approval.

When to use: Any time you spend money for work purposes — e.g. buying supplies, printing costs, petty cash items. Record it the same day it happens.

Step-by-step: Adding a New Expense

1

Open the Expenses screen

Tap Expenses from the side menu. The screen shows all expenses you have submitted this month, with a summary at the top: Total, Approved, Pending.

2

Tap + New Expense

The expense form appears.

3

Fill in the expense details

Reason (Expense account): Select the category from the dropdown (e.g. Stationery, Cleaning Supplies).
Pay from: Select the payment source (e.g. Cash, InstaPay).
Amount: Enter the amount paid.
Expense date: Defaults to today. Change if needed.
Remarks: Optional — add a short note explaining what was purchased.

4

Tap "Submit for Approval"

The expense is saved and sent to your manager for review. You will see a confirmation: "Expense submitted for approval". The expense appears in your list with a Pending Approval badge.

5

Manager approves

Once your manager approves the expense, the badge changes to Approved and the amount appears under the Approved total in the summary.

Viewing and Filtering Expenses

Month filter: By default the screen shows the current month. Tap the month selector to switch to a previous month.

Filter by payment method: Tap the filter icon to show only expenses paid from a specific payment source.

Pull to refresh: Swipe down on the expense list to refresh it from the server.

Expense statuses: Pending Approval → awaiting manager review; Approved → confirmed; Draft → saved but not yet submitted.

💡 Tips

  • Submit expenses on the same day — do not accumulate them for later.
  • Always select the correct payment source so your manager can review the expense correctly.
  • If you do not see the correct expense reason in the list, contact your manager to add it to the system.
  • Drafts are saved locally — if you close the app mid-form, the draft is preserved when you reopen it.

⚠️ Common Issues

  • "No payment sources available": Your branch has no payment methods configured for expenses. Contact your manager.
  • "Enter a valid amount": The amount field is empty or contains invalid characters. Enter a number (e.g. 50 or 50.5).
  • Expense stuck in Pending: Your manager has not yet approved it. Follow up with them directly.
