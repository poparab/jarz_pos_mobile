Login & Profile

How to sign into the app, select the correct mode, and manage your profile.

When to use: At the start of every shift and whenever you need to log out or update your password.

Step-by-step

1

Open the POS web app

Go to the URL provided by your manager (e.g. https://erp.orderjarz.com/pos/). The login screen appears automatically.

2

Enter your username and password

Type your credentials as given by your manager. Tap Login.

3

Choose Login Mode: Employee

A dialog asks you to choose between Line Manager and Employee. Staff must always choose Employee. This mode requires opening a shift before you can use the POS.

4

Your POS profile loads automatically

Because your account is assigned to exactly one POS profile (branch), the app selects it for you. You will not see a profile-selection screen.

5

You are redirected to Start Shift

The app will not let you use the POS until a shift is open. Proceed to Start Shift.

Accessing your Profile

A

Open the side menu

Tap the ☰ Menu icon (top-right) on the Kanban or POS screen, then tap Profile.

B

Change password or log out

The profile screen shows your name, role, and linked POS profile. Use the Logout button to sign out. Contact your manager to reset your password.

💡 Tips

  • Always choose Employee at login — never Line Manager.
  • Do not share your password with other staff members.
  • If the app opens on a blank screen, refresh the browser tab.

⚠️ Common Issues

  • Wrong password: The app shows an error. Re-type carefully — passwords are case-sensitive. Contact your manager if locked out.
  • Blank or spinning screen after login: Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page.
  • Redirected to "No POS profile": Your account has not been assigned a POS profile. Contact your manager.
