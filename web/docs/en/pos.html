POS Order Entry

How to build a cart, add a customer, set delivery or pickup, choose payment, and submit an order.

When to use: Every time a customer places a new order — in-person, over the phone, or online. A shift must be open before you can submit orders.

Step-by-step

1

Go to the POS screen

After starting your shift, the app opens on the POS screen. You can also tap Point of Sale from the side menu at any time.

2

Select the customer first

Before adding any product, tap Add Customer. Search by name or phone number, then select the customer from the results. If the customer has a delivery address on file, it loads automatically. A customer must be selected before products are added or the order is submitted.

3

Search for items and add to cart

After the customer is selected, type an item name in the search bar or scroll the product grid. Tap an item to add it to the cart. Tap the item again (or use the +/− buttons in the cart) to adjust quantity. You can also select Bundles if configured for your profile.

4

Choose Delivery or Pickup

Delivery: Tap Select Delivery Time to choose an available time slot from the list. Delivery charges are added automatically based on the customer's address.
Pickup: Toggle the Pickup from Branch (no delivery fee) option. The delivery slot selector disappears and no shipping fee is applied.

5

Review the cart total

The cart shows: items subtotal + delivery fee (if applicable) = Grand Total. Remove items with the trash icon, or clear the entire cart with Clear Cart.

6

Choose a payment method and tap Submit

A dialog asks you to select: Cash, InstaPay, Wallet, or another method configured for your branch. After selecting, the order is submitted to the server and the invoice is created.

7

Order confirmed — start a new one

A success message appears with the invoice name (e.g. ACC-SINV-2026-XXXXX). The cart clears automatically and you are ready for the next order. The new invoice appears on the Kanban Board.

Drafts (Auto-save)

The POS automatically saves your cart every few seconds as a draft. If you accidentally close the app or switch screens, your cart is restored when you return. To view and restore saved drafts, tap the Drafts icon (clock/history icon) in the POS header.

Drafts are stored on your device. If you log out, drafts for your session may be cleared — always submit orders before logging out.

💡 Tips

  • Always select the customer before adding products — this ensures the correct address, delivery charge, and customer history.
  • If no delivery slots appear, contact your manager — the branch timetable may need configuration.
  • For pickup orders, always toggle the Pickup option; otherwise the order will be treated as delivery.
  • If you make a mistake after submission, go to the Kanban Board and use the Edit Invoice (amendment) option.

⚠️ Common Issues

  • "Please select a delivery time": You tapped Submit without selecting a delivery slot. Choose a slot first or toggle Pickup.
  • Cannot add products / customer required: Select the customer first, then add items to the cart.
  • Items not loading: Check internet connection and refresh the page. If the profile has no items configured, contact your manager.
  • Submit button greyed out: The cart is empty. Add at least one item.
  • Order submitted but not visible on Kanban: Refresh the Kanban board by tapping the refresh icon.
