Shift Start & End

Open your shift at the start of the day and close it at the end. Both actions record cash balances for accounting.

When to use: Every working day — Start Shift before your first order, End Shift after your last order.

Start of Day Starting Your Shift

1

The app opens on the Start Shift screen

Right after login (Employee mode), you are taken to Start Shift automatically. You cannot use the POS until the shift is open.

2

Enter the cash drawer opening balance only

Count only the physical cash in your drawer for this BOS/POS profile and type that amount. Staff do not enter InstaPay, mobile wallet, bank, or other digital-account balances during shift start. If you see a non-cash field and are unsure what to do, leave it unchanged/zero and contact your manager.

3

Tap "Start Shift"

The app sends the opening balance to the server and shows "Shift Active". You are then redirected to the POS order screen.

End of Day Ending Your Shift

1

Finish all pending orders first

Before closing, confirm that all orders for the day are in the correct state on the Kanban board. Settle any outstanding payments.

2

Open the End Shift screen

From the POS or Kanban screen, tap the ☰ Menu → End Shift. You can also tap "Go to End Shift" if shown on the shift status banner.

3

Review the shift summary

The screen shows the total sales and a breakdown of invoices for the shift. For staff reconciliation, focus on the cash drawer amount for your BOS/POS profile.

4

Enter the cash drawer closing balance only

Count the physical cash in your drawer and type the actual amount under Closing Amount. Do not enter InstaPay, mobile wallet, bank, or other digital-account balances. The app calculates the difference (variance) between expected and actual cash automatically.

5

Tap "End Shift"

Confirms the closing balance. The app shows "Shift ended successfully." If there is a cash discrepancy, a journal entry is automatically created. The shift is now archived.

💡 Tips

  • Count the cash drawer physically before typing the opening or closing amount — do not guess.
  • Do not reconcile InstaPay, mobile wallet, or bank balances as a staff user; those balances are handled by managers/accounting.
  • If you see a "Shift Already Open" warning, another user has already opened a shift on the same profile. Contact your manager.
  • The shift must be open for orders to be submitted from the POS screen.

⚠️ Common Issues

  • "No active shift found": You are on the End Shift screen but the shift was never started. Go back and start a shift first.
  • Shift start fails: Check internet connectivity. If the error persists, contact your manager to check if a shift is already open for your profile.
  • Large variance / difference: Recount the cash. If the discrepancy is real, inform your manager before submitting.
